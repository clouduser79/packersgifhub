ABOUT ME

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Shawn, the creator behind Packers Pain. I'm a digital designer and GIF artist. I started making GIFs in August as a fun way to combine my love for sports, meme culture, and digital art. Over time, it grew into a creative outlet where I could share my unique take on the highs and lows of being a sports fan — especially when it comes to the Green Bay Packers. Of course I make other GIFs but my main goal is for the Packers and others to recognize my work someday. This effort was not all done by myself. Most of my GIFs are memes I created myself, however there are some people who have given me ideas or even full on memes. I won't point out any names, but thank you to all of you! You have been a big part of why we came so far! Shoutout to @Green.Bay.Packers, @mckinney.mafia, @nocontextpackers, @packers.base, and @depressedpackersfan_ for being some of my biggest supporters and giving me feedback on my work. I am grateful for all of you and I hope to continue making content that you all enjoy! The images on the left are some of their instagram accounts as well as @tfc.gb, so go follow them!

What I Create

My content is a mix of sports GIFs, reaction content, and meme culture. I love creating GIFs that capture the emotions of being a sports fan — the excitement, the frustration, the humor. Whether it's a hilarious reaction to a game moment or a clever meme about the latest team drama, I aim to make content that resonates with fellow fans and adds some fun to their day. I also enjoy experimenting with different styles and formats, from simple reaction GIFs to more complex animations that tell a story or make a statement. Ultimately, my goal is to create content that brings joy and laughter to people's feeds while showcasing my unique perspective as a Packers fan.
